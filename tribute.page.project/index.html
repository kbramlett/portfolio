About Winky

Winky is a Jack Russell and Chihuahua mix.

Winky was adopted from the Central Oklahoma Humane Society in October 2018.

She has a lot of energy sometimes, and likes to try to run away. This is no bueno.

She likes to chew on bones and bask in the sun.
